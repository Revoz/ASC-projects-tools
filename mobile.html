Motor selection (IEC 60034 / 60072)

Gearbox selection

Gearbox family rules per SEW-Eurodrive / Nord / Sumitomo catalog practice: inline helical for i ≤ 60, helical-bevel for right-angle or i ≤ 110, planetary above ≈ 20 kN·m output torque. Service factor per AGMA 6013 for agitator duty (24 h, moderate shock).

Shaft design (ASME combined loading)

Method: d³ = (16/π·τall)·√[(KbM)² + (KtT)²]; hydraulic side load Fh ≈ 19000·PkW/(N·D) per Fasano/Chemineer practice; lateral critical speed of cantilever with end mass, operating limit ≤ 0.65·Nc. Axial thrust for helical ribbon per Bortnikov et al. (1973): Pax = K₂·μ·n·dm².

Mounting / fixation on tank

Mounting flange per DIN 28137-2 agitator nozzle series; beam mounting per vendor practice above 15 kW or shaft > DN80 nozzle capability. Gusseted pads on head with load spreading ≥ 3× flange OD footprint.

General arrangement / fabrication drawing

CIP spray devices (ASME BPE)

Coverage rule: 2.5–3.0 US gpm per foot of vessel circumference ≈ 31–37 L/min per meter (ASME BPE SD-3.6 practice / Alfa Laval & Lechler sizing guides). Agitator internals create spray shadow → minimum two devices offset 180°, positioned to wet shaft and blade backsides. Static ball ≤ 3 bar; rotary jet for Ø > 3.5 m or heavy soils.

Process nozzles & vent

Line sizing at v ≤ 2.0 m/s inlet, ≤ 1.5 m/s gravity outlet. Vent sized for maximum air displacement (pump-in/out) + thermal breathing, concept per API 2000; hygienic tanks receive sterile air filter (0.2 µm) sized at ≤ 8 m/s face velocity.
